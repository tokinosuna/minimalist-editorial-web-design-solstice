The Story of 131c

131c is more than a name—it's a multi-layered cultural symbol, a concise story born from an unconscious stroke of genius.

The Root: 131°C

It begins with temperature, representing the scorching heat of Taipei, my hometown. This is the root of my creative fire, an essence of passion and origin.

The Taste: PANTONE 131 C

It's also an official color code (#F2F0A1), lending an inherent authority in design and aesthetics. This soft, warm yellow balances the 'heat' with warmth, creativity, and what can be called 'the temperature of inspiration'.

The Vision: C-131 Aircraft

Finally, it echoes the C-131 airplane, symbolizing a global vision, exploration, and the intersection of technology and travel. It represents the journey of taking ideas and soaring with them across the world.

Together, these elements form a brand that is rooted in passion, guided by taste, and limitless in its vision. It is not just a name I chose, but a destiny that chose me.
